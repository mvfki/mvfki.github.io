Problem 1.2

Pseudocode for folding the clothings:

bottoms = {pants, jeans, shorts, ...}
tops = {t_shirt, shirt, coat, ...}
allClothings = [pants, jeans, shirt1, shirt2, ...]
for each item in allClothings:
    lay the item 
    if item belongs to bottoms:
        fold from the crotch
        fold from the knee
    else if item belongs to tops:
        fold the left sleeve
        fold the right sleeve
        fold from stomach
    put the item into the wardrobe
