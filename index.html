ハチミツとクローバー // Honey And Clover // 蜂蜜与四叶草

Links: IMDb 7.9/10 Crunchyroll 9.4/10 Bilibili 9.7/10

Yuuta Takemoto (竹本祐太), a sophomore at an arts college, shares a cheap apartment with two seniors—the eccentric Shinobu Morita (森田忍), who keeps failing to graduate due to his absenteeism, and the sensible Takumi Mayama (真山巧), who acts as a proper senior to Takemoto, often looking out for him.

Takemoto had not given much thought to his future until one fine spring day, when he meets the endearing Hagumi Hanamoto (花本はぐみ) and falls in love at first sight. Incredibly gifted in the arts, Hagumi enrolls in Takemoto's university and soon befriends the popular pottery student Ayumi Yamada (山田あゆみ). Ayumi is already well acquainted with the three flatmates and secretly harbors deep feelings for one of them.

Honey and Clover is a heartwarming tale of youth, love, soul-searching, and self-discovery, intricately woven through the complex relationships between five dear friends.
