ぼっち・ざ・ろっく！ / 孤独摇滚！ / Bocchi the Rock!

Links: IMDb 8.7/10 Crunchyroll 4.9/5

Yearning to make friends and perform live with a band, lonely and socially anxious Hitori "Bocchi" Gotou (後藤一里 ”ぼっち”) devotes her time to playing the guitar. On a fateful day, Bocchi meets the outgoing drummer Nijika Ijichi (伊地知虹夏), who invites her to join Kessoku Band (結束バンド) when their guitarist, Ikuyo Kita (喜多郁代), flees before their first show. Soon after, Bocchi meets her final bandmate—the cool bassist Ryou Yamada (山田涼).

Although their first performance together is subpar, the girls feel empowered by their shared love for music, and they are soon rejoined by Kita. Finding happiness in performing, Bocchi and her bandmates put their hearts into improving as musicians while making the most of their fleeting high school days.

- Written by MAL Rewrite on MyAnimeList
